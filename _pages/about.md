Cryptography researcher & engineer.

PhD from the University of Edinburgh 2022, [BTL](https://www.ed.ac.uk/informatics/blockchain)/[LFCS](https://www.ed.ac.uk/studying/postgraduate/degrees/index.php?r=site/view&id=493), working on zero-knowledge proofs, advised by [Markulf Kohlweiss](https://scholar.google.ru/citations?user=pOfF9ygAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao). Master's at [MPRI](https://wikimpri.dptinfo.ens-cachan.fr/doku.php), Bachelor's at ITMO University ([CT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ITMO_University#World_Programming_Championship) department).

Founder and CTO of Anafi, building privacy-preserving automation and delegation for DeFi. Founder of [Metajoin Ltd](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/SC846919), an IT consultancy company specialising in cryptography-focused R&D, currently working closely with [Clique](https://clique.com/). Ex-researcher / ZK engineer in [o1Labs](https://o1labs.org/), worked on [Ensue/Project Untitled](https://github.com/o1-labs/ensue-whitepaper/blob/main/whitepaper.pdf) within the [Mina](https://minaprotocol.com/) ecosystem. While in Edinburgh I was a research consultant at [IOG/IOHK](https://iohk.io/), and did a research internship at [Ethereum Foundation](https://ethereum.foundation/). I was a part of the [Serokell](https://serokell.io/) team in 2015-2018 working on several blockchain-related projects including the original [Cardano SL](https://github.com/input-output-hk/cardano-sl/) for IOHK. My area of software development expertise then was mostly haskell ecosystem related, and I'm still a big haskell fan!

For recent professional activity see the [o1Labs crypto](https://o1labs.notion.site/crypto-reading-group) [seminar](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKIvwYrcKk8CjcmmYHwam_m1Yi0pw6pzB), my [blog](/blog) and [publications](/publications). I also do a bit of music, see my youtube and soundcloud!
